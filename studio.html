Studio

Soft industrial calm

Voltex Studio is an interior design practice working across Europe on homes, hospitality, and boutique workspaces — always with a measured, human scale.

For over two decades we have developed a language of quiet interiors: soft industrial lines, subtle texture, and lighting that guides rather than shouts. Each object and material is chosen with intention, so your spaces feel calm, generous, and precise.

Our process moves from research and architectural planning, through 3D visualisation and lighting strategy, to on‑site art direction and final styling. We collaborate closely with architects, craftspeople, and suppliers to protect the concept at every stage.

We work best with clients who value depth over speed, understand that fewer, better decisions create enduring spaces, and are ready to invest in details that you feel every day rather than notice once.

Services
Interior concepts and planning, bespoke furniture design, lighting and acoustic strategy, FF&E selection, styling, and art direction on site.

Regions
Projects realised in Poland, Germany, Denmark, France, Spain, and the UK. We are used to remote collaboration and mixed on‑site / online oversight.
